About Us

Institute of Engineering and Technology is located in the University Campus. It is 400 metres away from N.H.17 in the Kohinoor-Kondotty road. Distance from Calicut city is 24 km. The nearest railway station is Parappanangadi which is 12km away from the Campus. The Feroke railway station is located at a distance of 13 km from here. Both the stations are in the Shoranur-Mangalore route. Express trains stop at both stations. Kozhikode Airport is only 8 km away from the Institute. Being located on the National Highway, Campus is well connected by bus.
